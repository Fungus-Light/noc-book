献给我的grandmother, Bella Manel Greenfield (1915.8.13 -- 2010.4.3)

Bella Manel 出生在纽约市. 一位数学领域的先锋, she earned her PhD in 1939 from New York University under the supervision of Richard Courant. She worked for Ramo-Wooldridge (now TRW) and at the Rand Corporation with Richard Bellman. Later, she taught mathematics at the College of Notre Dame (now Notre Dame de Namur University) in Belmont, California, and at UCLA. The Bella Manel Prize for outstanding graduate work by a woman or minority was established at NYU’s Courant Institute in 1995.
